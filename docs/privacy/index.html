Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Overview

Watashiwomite is a free, open-source Android app that syncs your MyAnimeList (MAL) library with a Sonarr instance that you own and operate. We take your privacy seriously. This policy describes what data the app collects, where it goes, and what we never do.

Data We Collect

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your MAL OAuth2 access and refresh tokens (used to fetch your anime list)
  • Your MAL username (displayed in the app)
  • Your Sonarr URL and API key (used to add/query series)
  • Your TVDB API key (used to look up series metadata)
  • Sync history and results (cached locally for the dashboard)
  • App preferences (episode filters, quality profiles, etc.)

None of this data is transmitted to any server we operate.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates directly with the following third-party services on your behalf:

  • MyAnimeList (myanimelist.net) — to authenticate and retrieve your anime list
  • TVDB (thetvdb.com) — to search for series metadata and match anime titles
  • Sonarr — your self-hosted instance; data goes directly to your server
  • GitHub (api.github.com) — to check for app updates (release metadata only; no personal data sent)

Please review each service's own privacy policy for how they handle your data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry
  • We do not transmit your credentials or tokens to any server we control
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — required to communicate with MAL, TVDB, Sonarr, and GitHub
  • REQUEST_INSTALL_PACKAGES — used only when you choose to install an in-app update
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — used only to save the downloaded update APK to external files

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device using Android DataStore. You can clear all app data at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Watashiwomite → Clear Data. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
